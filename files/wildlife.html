Animal Categories:

The Big Five:

Elephant, Lion, Leopard, Buffalo, and Rhino roam freely in their natural habitat.

Birds:

Over 500 bird species, including ostriches, eagles, and kingfishers.

Crocodiles and various snake species found in rivers and bushlands.

Hirola antelope and endangered wild dogs.

Conservation Message:

We work tirelessly to protect our wildlife from threats like poaching, habitat loss, and climate change.
