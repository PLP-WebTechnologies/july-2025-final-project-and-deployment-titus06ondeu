Things to Do at Tsavo National Park

Experience the wild like never before with these exciting activities.

🚙 Game Drives

Enjoy early morning or evening drives to spot the Big Five and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

🐘 Wildlife Photography

Capture breathtaking moments of elephants, lions, and rare bird species in the golden African light.

🌄 Nature Walks

Take guided walks to learn about plants, tracks, and the smaller wonders of the savannah.

🏕 Camping

Spend a night under the stars with the sounds of the wild all around you.

🏞 Visit Lugard Falls

See the spectacular waterfalls along the Galana River and enjoy scenic views.
